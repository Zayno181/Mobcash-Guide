7. How to add subagent

  1. Find Someone to Work With You: Have a person ready to work as your sub-agent before starting registration.
  2. Inform Your Account Manager: Notify your account manager about the new sub-agent.
  3. Be Ready to Pay $50: A $50 payment is required to create and activate the sub-agent's account. This amount will later appear in your Web Management account. No sub-agent account can be opened without this payment.
  4. Fill out the Subagent Form: Complete in English with:
    • City, Region, Country
    • Name of Cash Desk
    • Geolocation (example: 32.533556342, 3.43567425268)
    • Currency
    • Full Name
    • Contact Number
    Also submit the sub-agent's ID card and a selfie with the ID for verification.
    [Example of ID and selfie]
  5. Sub-Agent MobCash Account: The sub-agent receives their own separate MobCash account.
    [Screenshot of sub-agent MobCash home with Limit and Balance]

Account Manager Support

All communication with subagents will be only through you, the master agent. The account manager will not communicate directly with the subagent.

Commission Structure

  • Sub-Agent Commissions (from players): X% on deposits, Y% on withdrawals.
  • As a master agent, you can take a portion of the sub-agent's commissions. Agree on the split before recruitment.

Example - Deposits: Player deposits $100. Subagent earns X% → $X. You can take for example 1/5 from that X, sub-agent keeps 4/5.

Example - Withdrawals: Player withdraws $100. Subagent earns Y% → $Y. You can take 1/2, leave 1/2 to subagent.

Web Management Platform

After the sub-agent is approved, your account manager will create a Web Management account for you. This system allows you to monitor sub-agent activities, track deposits/withdrawals, view total commissions, and manage transfers to MobCash.

All your deposits and monthly commissions will be sent to your Web Management account (including subagents' commissions). You can then transfer any amount to your own MobCash or your subagent's MobCash.

Transfer Steps

  1. Go to Eposes → Prepayment Structure
  2. Select Superagent
  3. Click on your name → Click Transfer
  4. Choose whether to transfer to yourself or your sub-agent
  5. Insert the amount
