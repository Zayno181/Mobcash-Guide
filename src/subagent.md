# 7. How to add subagent

<ol>
    <li><strong>Find Someone to Work With You:</strong> Have a person ready to work as your sub-agent before starting registration.</li>
    <li><strong>Inform Your Account Manager:</strong> Notify your account manager about the new sub-agent.</li>
    <li><strong>Be Ready to Pay $50:</strong> A $50 payment is required to create and activate the sub-agent's account. This amount will later appear in your Web Management account. No sub-agent account can be opened without this payment.</li>
    <li><strong>Fill out the Subagent Form:</strong> Complete in English with:
        <ul>
            <li>City, Region, Country</li>
            <li>Name of Cash Desk</li>
            <li>Geolocation (example: <code class="copyable">32.533556342, 3.43567425268</code>)</li>
            <li>Currency</li>
            <li>Full Name</li>
            <li>Contact Number</li>
        </ul>
        Also submit the sub-agent's ID card and a selfie with the ID for verification.
        <img src="/assets/id-selfie-example.png" alt="Example of ID card and selfie" class="guide-image">
    </li>
    <li><strong>Sub-Agent MobCash Account:</strong> The sub-agent receives their own separate MobCash account.
        <img src="/assets/subagent-mobcash-home.png" alt="Sub-agent MobCash home screen showing limit and balance" class="guide-image">
    </li>
</ol>

<section>
    <h2>Account Manager Support</h2>
    <p>All communication with subagents will be only through you, the master agent. The account manager will not communicate directly with the subagent.</p>
</section>

<section>
    <h2>Web Management Platform</h2>
    <p>After the sub-agent is approved, your account manager will create a Web Management account for you. This system allows you to monitor sub-agent activities, track deposits/withdrawals, view total commissions, and manage transfers to MobCash.</p>
    <p>All your deposits and monthly commissions will be sent to your Web Management account (including subagents' commissions). You can then transfer any amount to your own MobCash or your subagent's MobCash.</p>

    <h3>Transfer Steps</h3>
    <ol>
        <li>Go to Eposes → Prepayment Structure</li>
        <li>Select Superagent</li>
        <li>Click on your name → Click Transfer</li>
        <li>Choose whether to transfer to yourself or your sub-agent</li>
        <li>Insert the amount</li>
    </ol>
    <img src="/assets/web-management-transfer.png" alt="Web Management transfer interface" class="guide-image">
</section>
