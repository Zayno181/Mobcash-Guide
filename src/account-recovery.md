            <h1>3. Steps to recover a Reddy account</h1>

            <section>
                <h2>3.1. Introduction</h2>
                <p>The Reddy Program is the official communication channel between the agent and the account manager. It is used for work follow-up, support, handling requests, and performance updates. To ensure service quality, the program follows a policy related to activity and regular logins.</p>
            </section>

            <section>
                <h2>3.2. Reddy Program Closure Policy</h2>
                <p>The Reddy Program is automatically closed if the agent does not use it or show any activity for a continuous period of 15 days. Inactivity includes: not opening the program, not sending messages, and not interacting with the account manager.</p>
            </section>

            <section>
                <h2>3.3. What Happens When the Program Is Closed?</h2>
                <ul>
                    <li>Communication via Reddy will be temporarily suspended.</li>
                    <li>The agent will not be able to send or receive messages until reactivation.</li>
                    <li>Reopening the program may require identity verification to ensure the account belongs to the agent.</li>
                </ul>
            </section>

            <section>
                <h2>3.4. How to Reopen the Reddy Program</h2>
                <ol>
                    <li>Contact the verification team directly via Telegram: <a href="https://t.me/r_verification" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">t.me/r_verification</a></li>
                    <li>Information that may be requested: username or agent ID, proof of ownership (screenshot or confirmation), any additional info.</li>
                </ol>
                <p>Once the required details are sent, the request will be reviewed and the program will be reopened as soon as possible.</p>
            </section>

            <section>
                <h2>3.5. Advice for the Agent</h2>
                <p>We recommend opening the program and sending at least one message every 10-12 days to keep the program active and avoid automatic closure.</p>
            </section>
