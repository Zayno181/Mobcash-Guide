            <h1>5. Instructions on how to set up an autoreserve</h1>
            <ol>
                <li>Go to your main account on 1xBet.</li>
                <li>Take your user ID.</li>
                <li>Go to the payment section.</li>
                <li>Choose your preferred payment method.</li>
            </ol>
            <p>The funds will be transferred automatically to MobCash in the limit. As for the money transfer, it is an automatic process, and sometimes these transfers may take a little longer to appear.</p>
            <p>Once the transfer is completed, you will receive a message on your Reddy account similar to:</p>
            <div class="message-example">
                <code>DB Finance Bot<br>Debited from fingroup 13 015,59 DZDM (50,08 USD) transferred to the cashier 13 015,59 DZDM (50,08 USD)</code>
            </div>
            <p>If you receive the message and notice that the money has not arrived yet, try logging out and logging back in again. If it still does not appear, contact your account manager.</p>
