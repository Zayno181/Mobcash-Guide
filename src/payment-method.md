            <h1>6. The process for changing a payment method</h1>

            <section>
                <h2>Contact Your Account Manager in Reddy</h2>
                <p>You must first contact your account manager or support manager in Reddy. They will provide you with information about the available payment methods for your country and the fees or percentages associated with each method when depositing.</p>
            </section>

            <section>
                <h2>Choosing Your Payment Method(s)</h2>
                <p>You can select one or multiple payment methods. If you choose two or more methods, the commission applied will be calculated as the average of the fees for the selected methods:</p>
                <p><strong>For two methods:</strong> Commission = (Fee1 + Fee2) / 2</p>
                <p><strong>For three methods:</strong> Commission = (Fee1 + Fee2 + Fee3) / 3</p>
            </section>

            <section>
                <h2>Confirming and Applying the Payment Method</h2>
                <p>Once you have agreed, your account manager will submit a request to change or add the selected method to your account. You can check the status in your 1xBet account under "Deposit Methods".</p>
                <p><strong>Important:</strong> Make sure to confirm the available payment methods before making any deposits. The selected method(s) will affect the commission applied to your transactions.</p>
            </section>
