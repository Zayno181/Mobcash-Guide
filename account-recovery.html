3. Steps to recover a Reddy account

3.1. Introduction

The Reddy Program is the official communication channel between the agent and the account manager. It is used for work follow-up, support, handling requests, and performance updates. To ensure service quality, the program follows a policy related to activity and regular logins.

3.2. Reddy Program Closure Policy

The Reddy Program is automatically closed if the agent does not use it or show any activity for a continuous period of 15 days. Inactivity includes: not opening the program, not sending messages, and not interacting with the account manager.

3.3. What Happens When the Program Is Closed?

  • Communication via Reddy will be temporarily suspended.
  • The agent will not be able to send or receive messages until reactivation.
  • Reopening the program may require identity verification to ensure the account belongs to the agent.

3.4. How to Reopen the Reddy Program

  1. Contact the verification team directly via Telegram: t.me/r_verification
  2. Information that may be requested: username or agent ID, proof of ownership (screenshot or confirmation), any additional info.

Once the required details are sent, the request will be reviewed and the program will be reopened as soon as possible.

3.5. Advice for the Agent

We recommend opening the program and sending at least one message every 10-12 days to keep the program active and avoid automatic closure.
